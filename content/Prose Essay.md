# Prose Essay

3 parts of a thesis: How (technique), What (argument about idea) and So what (line of reasoning outside the text).

Evidence: Look for POV/narrative voice, diction/word choice, detail, tone and imagery, syntax, repetition, figurative language, and characterization.

Commentary should develop the what and so what. Look for complexity and nuance, this is true but this causes this. Evolution from this to this, change throughout the story. Simultaneously existing layers it is this but also this.

Characters are written this way because people and life is this way.

Organize:

Three ways in order of goodness

- Structure: How does the paragraph shift. When does the story shift. Split into 3 major shift.
- Device: break up by literary element, less sophisticated
- Hybrid: move by paragraph first then by device
