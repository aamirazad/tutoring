# Short Story

A short story is brief with a single significant episode (usually at the end)

More of a emotion effect than a clear cut message

Setting, plot, point of view, stylistic devices, tone, theme

Tone is the author's point of view, theme is a argument

When looking at setting, look at how the story would be in a different setting

Atmosphere is a feeling or mood created by the writer

Plot is a series of connected happenings and their result, conflict is the source of problems (man v \[…\]), think about when the author starts the story, is the plot logical

Diction is word choice (format or informal). Denotation is the dictionary definition. Connotations is the feeling attached to the word.

Tone is the attitude a writer takes towards a subject

Other devices: stream of consciousness is when the narrator just talks about their thoughts, figurative language, symbolism, allegory, and syntax

Characterization is how the writer develops a character; how they reveal feelings and appearance. Observe characters and the decisions they make to learn more about them. Characters could also be symbolic, satirical, realistic

Theme is the controlling insight, the author's main idea that they are trying to convey
